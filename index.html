Is this only available for Real Estate Brokers?

This service is only available to Real Estate Agents and Brokers. It is meant for established teams who can handle hundreds of home buyer leads per month.

Is it exclusive by territory?

Yes, it is exclusive. Many benefits of the platform would be diminished if we allowed territory overlap. There will only ever be one Listing Page Platform deployed in any one market area. Contact us to see if your territory is available.

How long does it take to get up and running?

It can take between 1 to 6 weeks. Each MLS has their own rules for gaining access to the active listings (IDX). We manage this entire process and it is usually the longest part of the entire setup.
